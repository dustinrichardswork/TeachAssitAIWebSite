#Slide: 4
#Header: Introduction to War
#Content: 
- Definition of war: a conflict between two or more groups
- Historical context and importance of war
- Examples of wars throughout history

#Slide: 5
#Header: Causes of War
#Content: 
- Political causes: territorial disputes, power struggles
- Economic causes: resources, trade routes
- Social causes: nationalism, ethnic tensions
- Ideological causes: religious or political beliefs
- Examples of wars caused by these factors

#Slide: 6
#Header: Different Types of War
#Content: 
- Conventional warfare: fought using conventional weapons and tactics
- Guerrilla warfare: irregular tactics, usually by small groups
- Civil war: fought within a country between different groups
- Proxy war: conflict between two major powers fought through smaller allies
- Modern warfare: includes cyber warfare and drone warfare

#Slide: 7
#Header: Major Wars in History
#Content: 
- World War I: causes, key events, and consequences
- World War II: causes, key events, and consequences
- Cold War: ideological conflict between the US and the Soviet Union
- Vietnam War: proxy war during the Cold War
- Other major wars from different time periods

#Slide: 8
#Header: The Impact of War
#Content: 
- Social impacts: loss of life, displacement, trauma
- Economic impacts: destruction, rebuilding, economic shifts
- Political impacts: changes in leadership, borders, ideologies
- Cultural impacts: changes in art, literature, and society
- Long-term effects of war on countries and the world
