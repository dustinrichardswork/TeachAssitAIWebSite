#Slide: 4
#Header: Introduction to Football
#Content: Football is a popular team sport, played globally, where two teams compete to score the most goals by maneuvering a ball into the opposing team's goal.

#Slide: 5
#Header: The Football Field
#Content: A football field, also known as a pitch, is finely marked with lines, circles, and goal boxes, which indicate where each team can and cannot move.

#Slide: 6
#Header: Ball Movement
#Content: Players predominantly use their feet to move the ball. They can also use other parts of their body, except for their hands.

#Slide: 7
#Header: Rules of Football Part 1
#Content: Each match lasts 90 minutes, divided into two halves. Offside, fouls, penalties are some common rules.

#Slide: 8
#Header: Rules of Football Part 2
#Content: At the end of a game, if the scores are equal, a tiebreaker through extra time or penalty shootouts can be used.

#Slide: 9
#Header: Positions in Football Part 1
#Content: A single team consists of Goalkeeper (Defensive), Defenders, Midfielders, Forwards (Offensive).

#Slide: 10
#Header: Positions in Football Part 2
#Content: Each position has a role. Say, defenders prevent opponents from scoring, while forwards try to score.

#Slide: 11
#Header: Football Strategies Part 1
#Content: Common strategies include attacking and defending, constantly changing based on game's progress.

#Slide: 12
#Header: Football Strategies Part 2
#Content: Examples of strategies: Counter Attack, Possession Play, High Press, Long Ball etc.

#Slide: 13
#Header: Football Competitions
#Content: There are several national and international football competitions like World Cup, UEFA Champions League.

#Slide: 14
#Header: Famous Footballers
#Content: Some globally renowned footballers include Lionel Messi, Cristiano Ronaldo, and Neymar.

#Slide: 15 
#Header: The Impact of Football
#Content: Beyond a game, football fosters team spirit, decision-making, and physical wellbeing.

#Slide: 16
#Header: How to get involved?
#Content: Join local clubs, schools' teams or independently through casual games to get involved in football.
